Cadillac Ad

90's Aesthetics

Clorox Can

Bespi

This is a watercolor on canvas piece emulating the visually captivating style of an advertisement. An vague product named "Mac", a common name for many desirable products (McDonalds, MAC Makeup, Apple technology, etc...) alongside blatant acknowledgment of what is traditionally subliminal in consumer adverts.

MAC

Continuation of the previous piece with some vapor/synthwave influences.

MAC 2

Marlboro Soup
