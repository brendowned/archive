Jeffrey Dahmer
Jeffrey Dahmer was a cannibalistic serial murderer and rapist in Ohio and Wisconsin from 1978-1991. Born to Lionel H Dahmer and Joyce A Dahmer in West Allis, WI May 21st 1960, Jeff was raised in a fairly normal middle class family until being left alone to his Akron home at the age of 18 in June 1978.
June 1978 marks the end of his innocence as a morally stable person, not for having graduated out of highschool and into adulthood, but for tainting his home, his life, as well as many others' lives with the murder of his first victim Steven Hicks. This is where all empathy should end for Dahmer, who will kill again 9 years later in 1987.
